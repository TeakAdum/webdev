# 🐾 Pet Cafe Simulator

An entertaining idle game where you manage a cafe with adorable pets!

## ✨ New Features Added

### 1. **Multiselect Pet Deletion** 📋
- Click the **"📋 Multiselect"** button to enter selection mode
- Click on individual pets to select them (they highlight in blue)
- Selected pets count is displayed
- Click **"🗑️ Delete Selected"** to remove all selected pets at once
- You get a **full refund** of their costs when deleted!

### 2. **Progressive Pet Pricing** 💰
- Each pet you attract gets **progressively more expensive**
- **Cost Formula**: Base cost $50 × 1.5^(number of pets)
  - 1st pet: $50
  - 2nd pet: $75
  - 3rd pet: $112
  - 10th pet: $1,295
  - 20th pet: $37,970

### 3. **Pet Income System** 💸
- **More expensive pets = More income over time**
- Each pet generates passive income automatically:
  - Income = 5% of pet cost per second
  - A $100 pet makes $5/second
  - A $500 pet makes $25/second
- Total income accumulates automatically every second

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Attract Pets**: Click "🐕 Attract Pet" to bring pets to your cafe
2. **Build Your Cafe**: The more pets you have, the more money you make passively
3. **Manage Pets**: Use multiselect to remove pets you don't want and reclaim their costs
4. **Upgrade**: Buy upgrades and decorations to improve your cafe
5. **Unlock New Pets**: Save up to unlock rare pets like dragons and unicorns

## 📊 Game Mechanics

- **Passive Income**: Pets automatically generate money based on their cost
- **Multiselect Mode**: Easily manage large collections of pets
- **Pet Happiness**: Happier pets generate better returns (indirectly through better interactions)
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Getting expensive pets requires strategic gameplay
- **Refund System**: Delete unwanted pets and get full refunds

## 💡 Strategy Tips

- Early game: Attract cheap pets for steady income
- Mid game: Save up for expensive pets that generate huge passive income
- Late game: Use multiselect to manage 50+ pets efficiently
- Mix of pet costs: Balance cheap fast-income pets with expensive high-value pets
